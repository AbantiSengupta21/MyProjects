Schooling

My schooling is from the school : Debiswari Vidyayatan from nursery class to class 12. I've passed Higher Secondary (class 12 final) examination on Pure Science in the year 2018 from Debiswari. There are three divisions of our school :

  • Debiswari Shishuniketan(nursery to KG-2)
  • Debiswari Shikhshaniketan(class-1 to class-5)
  • Debiswari Vidyaniketan(class-6 to class-12)

The school is situated at Makhla, an well-known girls' school.
